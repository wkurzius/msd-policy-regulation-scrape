ELANOnline District Regulations Header Manville Board of Education Home To Policy Search District Regulations District Regulations TOC District Regulation 2200 - CURRICULUM CONTENT Section: Program Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: July 2024 M Courses of study and instructional materials and programs shall be designed to eliminate discrimination on the basis of any of the protected categories listed at N.J.A.C. 6A:7-1.1(a) and promote understanding and mutual respect between children. The Superintendent or designee shall develop a procedure to address and eliminate any possible bias in the curriculum.
