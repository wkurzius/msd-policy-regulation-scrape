ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 3143 - DISMISSAL Section: Teaching Staff Members Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The Board of Education will enter a contract with each nontenured teaching staff member providing, in part, for the termination of employment by either party on proper notice in accordance with Board Policy No. 3124. The Board may dismiss a nontenured teaching staff member when dismissal is in the best interest of the school district. Termination notice will be duly given in writing and will state the reason therefore. However, the Board reserves the right to terminate a nontenured employee without notice when sufficient cause warrants. The Board will determine whether to permit an employee to continue to perform services during the period between the giving of notice and the date of termination. N.J.S.A. 18A:6-30; 18A:6-30.1; 18A:27-9 Adopted: 20 October 2009
