ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 3322 - STAFF MEMBER’S USE OF CELLULAR TELEPHONES Section: Teaching Staff Members Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: December 2017 The Manville Board of Education recognizes a permanent/temporary staff member may need to make a personal telephone call during their workday when the telephone call cannot be made before the permanent/temporary staff member reports to work and/or after the permanent/temporary staff member’s workday has concluded. A personal telephone call by a permanent/temporary staff member on their personal cellular telephone shall not be made while the permanent/temporary staff member is performing assigned school district responsibilities. A personal telephone/cell phone is not the acceptable communication vehicle between district staff members and students via either phone call or text.
