ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 4415 - SUBSTITUTE WAGES Section: Support Staff Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 In order to ensure reliable assistance in the absence of regular support staff employees, the Board of Education will offer competitive compensation to qualified substitute secretaries, clerks, custodians, maintenance workers, bus drivers, teacher aides, and cafeteria workers. In no instance shall the wages paid a substitute exceed the wages paid the regular employee. Substitute support staff members will be paid at a per diem rate set by the Board. Adopted: 20 October 2009
