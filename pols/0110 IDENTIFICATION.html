ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 0110 - IDENTIFICATION Section: Bylaws Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: February 2023 Name The official name of the Board of Education shall be “The Board of Education of Manville in the County of Somerset.” Purpose The Board of Education exists for the purpose of providing a thorough and efficient system of free public education in grades Pre-Kindergarten through twelve in the Manville School District. Composition The Manville School District is comprised of all the area within the municipal boundaries of Manville in the County of Somerset. Classification The school district shall be classified as a Type II district. Address The address of the Board of Education shall be: 1100 Brooks Boulevard Manville, New Jersey 08835 N.J.S.A. 18A:8-1; 18A:9-2; 18A:9-3; 18A:10-2 Adopted: 20 October 2009 Revised: 28 February 2023
