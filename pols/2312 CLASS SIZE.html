ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 2312 - CLASS SIZE Section: Program Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The maximum class size for grades one through twelve, excluding physical education, health, and non-academic electives shall, whenever possible, be twenty-five pupils; the minimum class size for secondary grades nine through twelve shall be nine pupils on the roster. Exceptions to this policy must be reported with a rationale by the Principal to the Superintendent so that the Board of Education may approve such exceptions for the subsequent school year. Special education, basic skills, and ESL classes are excluded from this regulation. The maximum/minimum class size for grades Kindergarten through eight shall be reported by the Principal to the Superintendent by September so that the Board of Education may review these exceptions at the September meeting. The maximum/minimum class size for secondary grades nine through twelve shall be reported by the Principal to the Superintendent by October so that the Board of Education may review these exceptions at the October meeting. Adopted: 20 October 2009
