ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 1130 - STAFF LIAISON COMMITTEES Section: Administration Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The Board of Education encourages the Superintendent to maintain close liaison with staff members in order to coordinate district programs and operations, to consult with appropriate staff members in developing administrative regulations and formulating recommendations for Board consideration, and to detect and resolve problems as they may arise. The Superintendent is authorized to establish such staff liaison committees as he/she may deem necessary. No staff liaison committee can be delegated the authority to make decisions or take action that is reserved to the Board or the Superintendent. Committee reports and recommendations may be advisory only. Adopted: 20 October 2009
