ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 0153 - ANNUAL APPOINTMENTS Section: Bylaws Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2012 The Board of Education may annually appoint the following positions: 1. A Board Secretary, N.J.S.A. 18A:17-2, 17-5; 2. A Treasurer of School Moneys, N.J.S.A. 18A:17-31; 3. A public school accountant, N.J.S.A. 18A:23-1; 4. A medical inspector, N.J.S.A. 18A:40-1; 5. A psychological examiner, N.J.S.A. 18A:46-11; 6. A member to serve as delegate to the New Jersey School Boards Association, N.J.S.A. 18A:6-46; 7. An attendance officer, N.J.S.A. 18A:38-32; 8. A member to serve as delegate to the Somerset County School Boards Association; 9. An attorney; and 10. An insurance broker. Adopted: 20 October 2009 Revised: 16 October 2012
