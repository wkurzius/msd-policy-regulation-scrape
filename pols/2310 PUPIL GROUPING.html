ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 2310 - PUPIL GROUPING Section: Program Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The Board of Education recognizes its responsibility to provide each child with the educational program and facility which will be of maximum benefit to him/her. The Board, therefore, authorizes the Superintendent to assign each child to the school and grade level which will best meet the needs of the pupil. The Superintendent is directed to devise such evaluation measures and placement procedures as will insure effective pupil grouping. When a pupil enters the Manville schools from another district, he/she will be placed in accordance with his/her ability and achievement. Adopted: 20 October 2009
