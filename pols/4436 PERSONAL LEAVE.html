ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 4436 - PERSONAL LEAVE Section: Support Staff Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The Board of Education will provide for an employee's compensated absence for reason of personal necessity not covered by the terms of a negotiated agreement or in an individual contract with the Board. The Board reserves the right to determine the reasons for which personal leave will be granted, the number of days that may be used in any one school year for personal leave, and the manner of proof of personal necessity. N.J.S.A. 18A:30-7 Adopted: 20 October 2009
