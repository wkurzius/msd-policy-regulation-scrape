The Board encourages the certified staff to develop criteria and procedures for presenting letters or other suitable awards to pupils for scholarship and distinguished service in any school activity. In all cases, the relationship between the award and the relevant goal or goals of the schools should be pointed out. The Board itself, will recognize special achievements of district pupils at public Board of Education meetings. Scholarships The Board permits scholarships to be given to the deserving pupils by civic organizations and other groups approved by the Superintendent. However, the Board directs that no pupil information be released without the pupil's permission (if eighteen or older) or that of his/her parent(s) or legal guardian(s) if pupil is under eighteen years of age. Should the donors wish to have the recipient named during appropriate ceremonies, they shall comply with the following policy: 1. The type of scholarship to be offered and the rules and regulations therefore shall be submitted to the Principal for approval. 2. The Superintendent, his/her staff and others designated by the Principal shall provide information to the sponsoring organization. 3. No data of a personal nature shall be displayed or made known to anyone not on the school staff without permission of the parent(s) or legal guardian(s) or pupil (if over eighteen years of age). Academic Recognition The Board values excellence and wishes to inculcate in pupils the desire to do their best in all things. It shall be the policy of this Board, therefore, to recognize outstanding academic accomplishment through the use of an honors and high honors list in grades three through twelve and an Honor Society in grades eleven and twelve. Athletic Participation The district shall recognize outstanding achievement in athletic endeavors in accordance with district regulations. Service Participation School service groups shall be recognized for participation in order to emphasize the importance of good citizenship in school life as outlined in the course of study.
