The Board may, as the opportunity or need arises, join with _X__ the local municipal governing body _X_ each or all of the governing bodies of municipalities comprising the school district _X_ the Board of County Commissioners of the county in acquiring, improving, equipping, operating, or maintaining jointly used facilities in accordance with applicable law.
