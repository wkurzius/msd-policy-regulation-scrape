ELANOnline District Policies Header Manville Board of Education Home To Regulation Search District Policies District Policies TOC District Policy 3433 - VACATIONS Section: Teaching Staff Members Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009 The Board of Education believes that the school district benefits when teaching staff members employed to work twelve months a year are given periodic relief from the responsibilities of their positions without loss of compensation. The Board reserves the right to determine the conditions under which vacation time may be taken when not otherwise covered by the terms of a negotiated agreement or in an individual contract with the Board. N.J.S.A. 18A:30-7 Adopted: 20 October 2009
